Easy Sausage Gravy and Biscuits

Description

Easy Sausage Gravy and Biscuits is a classic Southern comfort breakfast that combines warm, flaky buttermilk biscuits with a rich and creamy sausage gravy. Made with savory pork sausage, milk, and simple pantry ingredients, this hearty dish delivers a satisfying balance of flavors and textures that has made it a favorite for generations.

Perfect for busy mornings or a relaxing weekend brunch, this recipe comes together in just a few minutes while still offering the comforting taste of a homemade meal. Served fresh and piping hot, sausage gravy and biscuits are a timeless breakfast that brings warmth, simplicity, and plenty of flavor to the table.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) can refrigerated jumbo buttermilk biscuits
  • 1 (9.6 ounce) package Jimmy Dean® Original Hearty Pork Sausage Crumbles
  • ¼ cup flour
  • 2 ½ cups milk
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Arrange biscuits 1 to 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 13 to 15 minutes.

Step 3

Meanwhile, cook sausage in a large skillet over medium heat until thoroughly heated, stirring frequently, about 5 to 6 minutes.

Step 4

Stir in flour until well combined.

Step 5

Gradually add milk, stirring continuously, until the gravy thickens and comes to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer and stir for 2 more minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Step 6

Split biscuits in half. Place 2 halves on each of 8 plates; top with about 1/3 cup gravy.
